RTSP Camera Connection

Connect to Your RTSP Camera

This tool allows you to connect to your RTSP camera and view the stream in your browser.

  1. Enter your RTSP URL (e.g., rtsp://192.168.1.100:554/stream1)
  2. Enter your username and password if required
  3. Click "Connect" to start the stream
  4. Click "Disconnect" when you're done

Note: This tool uses MediaMTX to convert RTSP to HLS for browser viewing.
